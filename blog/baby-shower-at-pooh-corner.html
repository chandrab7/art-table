BABY SHOWER

The Baby Shower at Pooh Corner

A Winnie the Pooh themed baby shower at Weymouth Country Club in Ohio with a "Hundred Acre Wood" aesthetic. The expecting parents wanted handcrafted over polished, and every detail reflected that warmth.

An original beehive centerpiece was created and childhood photos of both mom and dad were displayed. The sweets table combined family recipes with local favorites.

The parents will use these hand-lettered signs as part of their nursery decor.

Hand-lettered Pooh quotations held special meaning for the expecting parents. Guests played estimation games throughout the afternoon.

Guests tested their estimation skills, but Grandma Anne guessed the closest!
