Welcome & Gift Box Collection

Welcoming out of town wedding guests with a thoughtfully prepared box is a great way to set the stage for the festivities. Boxes are packaged according to theme, occasion, season, or client specifications.

The Cleveland Box

Local beverages including Great Lakes Oktoberfest and Christmas Ale, sports-themed chocolates, chocolate covered pretzels, popcorn, nuts, water, thank-you note, weekend itinerary, and local attractions info.

Bride's and Groom's Favorites Box

Handmade brownies and lemon squares, preferred snack foods, "his and her" favorite candies, and Pellegrino water.

Autumn Gift Box

Organic apples with caramel sauce, autumn trail mix, handmade s'mores bars, salted caramel chocolate, and sparkling apple cider.

Art of the Table also creates seasonal boxes, bridesmaids' gifts, and bride-specific gift collections. Each box is curated with love for every occasion.
