BRIDAL SHOWER

Sarah's Bubbly Brunch

This pretty bridal shower was fun for everyone despite the actual rain showers of the day! The hors d'oeuvres display included fresh floral arrangements alongside photographs of the bride and groom.

Guests received custom-blended olive oil paired with crusty baguettes with linens tied with sprigs of fresh rosemary. Indoor coffee and dessert service rounded out the celebration beautifully.

How many is too many sweets? Is there any such thing?

The bride and groom gave Aunt Irene and Uncle Rich extra hugs for hosting such a wonderful celebration.
