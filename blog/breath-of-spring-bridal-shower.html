BRIDAL SHOWER

A Breath of Spring Bridal Shower

This bridal shower at the home of Aunt Maureen featured stunning table settings that told a family story.

Notice the combinations of china. These settings were lent to the happy couple by their parents, grandparents and aunts and uncles.

Fresh flowers continued the spring vibe and accented the overall elegance of the celebration.

Fresh raspberries await mimosas in sugared flutes. Refreshing water and tasty tea were also on hand.

The bride and groom were very appreciative of Aunt Maureen for hosting such a beautiful and meaningful shower.
