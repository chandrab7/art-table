Meet the Team

Three friends bound by a love for distinctive details

Since 1999, we've been transforming life's most meaningful moments into beautifully styled experiences. What began as a passion for creating personalized details for friends and family quickly grew — through 20 years of personal referrals — into Art of the Table.
