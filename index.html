Meet Annie

Since 1999, Annie has been transforming life's most meaningful moments into beautifully styled experiences. What began as a passion for creating personalized details for friends and family quickly grew through personal referrals into Art of the Table.

Every event, gift box, and creation is crafted with an eye for detail and a heart for making people feel truly special. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Annie brings warmth, creativity, and distinctive style to every project.
